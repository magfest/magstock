MAGStock Food Service

Attendees who purchase the meal plan will be provided three meals a day for Friday and Saturday, in addition to Thursday dinner and Sunday breakfast, all served in the glorious Pavilion. Disposable plates/cups will be provided, but we STRONGLY suggest you bring your own cup.
Minimize waste, save the planet!

A Vegetarian option that doesn't suck is also available. Let us know your preference when registering!
